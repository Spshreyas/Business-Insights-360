# 📊 Business Insights 360

> **From raw data to business decisions — analyzing growth, gaps, and profitability across a global business.**

---

## 🧭 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end business analysis of **Atliq Hardwares**, designed to uncover how a rapidly growing company performs across:

- Customers  
- Markets  
- Financial health (P&L)  
- Time (monthly & yearly trends)  

Rather than just building reports, the focus is on answering a deeper question:

> **Is growth actually translating into sustainable business performance?**

---

## 🧠 Key Business Story

- 📈 Revenue scaled aggressively from **87.5M → 598.9M (2019–2021)**  
- 📉 However, profit margins dropped from **41.4% → 36.4%**  
- 🌍 All markets underperformed against targets (**~9% gap overall**)  
- 🧾 Growth is heavily driven by a few high-performing customers  

### 💡 Insight:
> Growth alone is not success — **efficiency and profitability define sustainability.**
